What's Unique About Our Facial Recognition System?

Mask scan:

The system can scan and identify if the person is wearing a mask. Ensure hygiene practices are followed without manual tracking.

Access control:

The facial recognition system fully integrates with access control hardware like doors, gates, and turnstiles. Therefore, if someone is found to be violating any rule, the system automatically won't allow access. No manual tracking is needed.
